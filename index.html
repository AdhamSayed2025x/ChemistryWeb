Forms of Corrosion Analysis

Supervised by Dr. Mohamed Hamed | 2025

1. Uniform Attack

Corrosion distributed uniformly over the entire exposed surface.

2. Galvanic Corrosion

Two dissimilar metals electrically connected in electrolyte.

3. Crevice Corrosion

Localized corrosion within crevices or shielded areas.

4. Pitting Corrosion

Extremely localized attack resulting in holes.

5. Intergranular Corrosion

Localized attack along grain boundaries.

6. Selective Leaching

Removal of one element from an alloy (Dezincification).

7. Erosion Corrosion

Acceleration of attack due to relative movement.

8. Stress Corrosion Cracking

Cracking by tensile stress and corrosive medium.
